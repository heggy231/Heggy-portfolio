Why learn to code and projects

Always in demand: Back in year 2009, I got laid off from my first job out of college as an accountant. While unemployed, I noticed there are many software engineer jobs vs. accounting jobs. So I decided to jump into tech to become a in-demand professional! Since then I never looked back.
Resources: So many great free community of helpers, local meetups full of teachers resources. I love learning with people. With so many meetups I attend, it feels like a real community. Meanwhile, with the skill, I can make the world a better place!
